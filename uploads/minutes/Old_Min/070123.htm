National AMS Membership

    -Join or renew your National AMS membership if you haven't done so already.  The cost is $15 per year and you also receive a BAMS magazine every month filled with many great articles as well as possible senior thesis ideas (It's never too early to start thinking about a senior thesis topic!).  Order forms are available in the AMS mailbox in Agron 3010 or go online to www.ametsoc.org.
